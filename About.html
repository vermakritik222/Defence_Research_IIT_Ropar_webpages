About us

Motto of IIT Ropar is to dispay out intellect ok the right path and vision is to be a trend setter among the technology universities born in this millinnium. With departments and over 180 faculty members in pursuit of excellences it resolved to become the global hub of next gen Defence and security research . IIT Ropar has and inherent strategic advantage being located in the vicinity of military commands. It also addresses the void in defense and defence related MSME in North India and promote a security industrial complex in North India. IIT Ropar is the first (and only)IIt with a C.E dedicated for Defence addressing the chasm existing between defence , academic and defence industries.
