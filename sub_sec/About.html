About us

IIT Ropar Motto is to deploy our intellect on the right path and vision is to be a trend setter among the technology universities born in this millennium. With 11 departments and over 180 faculty members in pursuit of excellence, it resolves to become the global hub of next gen Defence and Security Research. IIT Ropar has an inherent strategic advantage being located in the vicinity of military commands. It also addresses the void in defence and defence related MSMEs and promote a security industrial complex in North India.
IIT Ropar is the first (and only) IIT with a CoE dedicated for Defence addressing the chasm existing between defence, academia and defence industries.
